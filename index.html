Our Medical Faculties

Faculty of Medicine

The Faculty of Medicine - Delta University was established by Presidential Decree No. 546 of 2019 on October 21, 2019. The Faculty of Human Medicine at Delta University is the Faculty No. 6 in the university, which was established by Presidential Decree No. 147 of 2007. The Delta University College of Medicine seeks to be a distinguished educational institution in modern medical education and scientific research at the national and regional levels.

Faculty of Dentistry

The Faculty of Oral and Dental Medicine (established in 2011) is one of the Faculties of Delta University (DU). Built on an area of about 7200 m2; inside DU's main Campus, it is considered one of the largest private dental schools in EGYPT. The University is located on Costal International Road, Dakahlia, Egypt.

Faculty of Pharmacy

The Faculty of Pharmacy at Delta University of Science and Technology was established by Ministerial Resolution No. 198 of 2011. It seeks to be at the forefront of other university faculties through continuous development and updating of curricula, teaching and learning methods, and this is achieved through the application of quality assurance in scientific departments, and adherence to national academic standards.
